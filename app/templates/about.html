This app uses Google's voice recognition technology to transcribe speech in real time. Here's what sets it apart:


  • It's totally free, no strings attached.

  • It's designed to stay at work for as long as you need it to.

  • It's designed specifically to help the hard of hearing understand what's going on in a conversation.
{% include 'arrow-icons.html' %}Can I find this on another platform?

A similar Android app may be on the way. In the meantime, this will work on Android's Google Chrome browser, and it's easy to bookmark a web page in your home screen.

{% include 'arrow-icons.html' %}Am I being recorded?

Nothing in the code lets me record what you say or what the transcription is. The original source code is right here. The only third party is Google, since the audio is sent to its servers for processing.
